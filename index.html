=== Home ===

Ohana Ice & Treats is a premier ice cream shop serving Chicago's residents and visitors since 2018. With the most innovative shaved ice options, an extensive menu of ice cream flavors, and vegan options, we strive to create an environment that is comfortable and welcoming for everyone. Whether you're looking for an afterschool treat or a relaxing weekend outing with the family, we want you to leave our shop with a smile after every visit. We're looking forward to partnering with surrounding areas in order to continue providing the community with the highest-quality ice cream and best-tasting shaved ice in the city. We'd like to personally invite you to stop by for one of our signature treats today!
