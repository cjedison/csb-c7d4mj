Our Story

In 2018, our owners, Keynn and Kenneth Cain, invested everything they had in opening our little ice cream shop. It was a labor of love in the beginning to navigate the challenges of a budding business during the pandemic. However, we pushed through our challenges because we believe in providing our customers with memorable experiences with delectable treats.

Shaved ice originated in Hawaii, so in order to provide an authentic Hawaiian experience, we import all of our high quality syrup directly from the source. The name of our shop comes from the Hawaiian word for “family,” and we extend this sentiment to our patrons. At Ohana Ice & Treats, you come in as a stranger, but leave as family.

We pride ourselves in offering top-notch customer service and premium products of the highest quality. We are available for private parties and events and we look forward to introducing Chicago to our mobile ice cream truck very soon.

We know that everyone could use something sweet these days, and there’s nothing sweeter than our selection of 32 ice cream flavors, 28 shaved ice options, smoothies, shakes, and sundaes. Our topping choices include Oreos, rainbow sprinkles, hot fudge, gummy bears, caramel syrup, and more!.

You can contact us at (773) 253-8533 if you have any questions about our menu, or you can stop by our shop in the Chicagoland area. We always are looking forward to serving you!
